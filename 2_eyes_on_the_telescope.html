Constellations

A constellation is a group of stars in the sky.

The stars look like a picture, like an animal or a shape.

People give them names. For example, the Big Dipper is a shape like a spoon.

Constellations help people find stars in the sky.

Would you like to find them?:

  1. Tonight, wait for it to get dark.
  2. Find a place with little light.
  3. Try to find the Ursa Major or Orion in the sky.

Have fun looking for stars!

Sometimes things aren't what they seem. 

It is important to update our knowledge.

What is the colour of the Sun?

The Sun is not yellow, orange or red. It is white!

The atmosphere makes us to see the Sun in different colours (yellow or orange). 

The planets move around the Sun but, does the Sun move?

Yes! The Sun is also moving.

It rotates on its axis and revolves around the galaxy (Milky Way).
