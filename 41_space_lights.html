3. Describe your planet!

Now, you will write with a partner.

Read the example and write a similar description.

You can write about the planet or celestial body you like.

Follow the next steps:

1. Read the example

It is a celestial body. It’s white, grey or blue. It’s made up of ice and rocks. It revolves around the Sun. it approaches and moves away from the Sun.

I like comets because they are beautiful. 

2. Write the description

Use these sentence starters to help you:

  • Its name is…
  • It’s (a planet, a star, a satellite…).
  • It’s (colour) and (big/small).
  • It’s made up of (ice, rock, dust, gas…).
  • It’s the (first, second, third, fourth, fifth…) planet of the Solar System.
  • It’s near (Jupiter, Saturn, Uranus, etc.).

3. Present your description

You can show your description in different ways:

  1. With a drawing in your notebook or cardboard
  2. With a digital presentation on the computer
  3. With recycled materials
  4. Or anywhere you want

Learning from my mistakes

How did you do in this activity?

Did you make a mistake?

It is normal to make mistakes when learning something new.

Failure is part of learning.

To learn from your mistakes, follow these tips:

1. Examine the part where I have failed.

2. Look for ways to improve that mistake.

3. Try it again.

4. Understand that mistakes are important for learning.

Don't forget: when you make a mistake once, you learn for the next try.

Aprendiendo de mis errores

¿Cómo te ha ido en esta actividad?

¿Cometiste un error?

Es normal cometer errores cuando aprendes algo nuevo.

El fracaso es parte del aprendizaje.

Para aprender de tus errores, sigue estos consejos:

  1. Examina la parte donde cometí el error.
  2. Busca formas de mejorar ese error.
  3. Vuelve a intentarlo.
  4. Entiende que los errores son importantes para aprender.

No lo olvides: cuando cometes un error una vez, aprendes para el siguiente intento.
